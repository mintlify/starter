## Regions

We operate a global network of service endpoints strategically distributed across major regions worldwide. Our infrastructure is continuously expanding, with additional endpoints being deployed regularly to ensure you experience the lowest latency and fastest response times no matter where you are. By connecting to the endpoint nearest to your location, you can achieve optimal performance and reliability for your Solana RPC calls.

| Location           | Protocol  | Endpoint                        |
|---------------|-------|---------------------------------|
| 🇺🇸 New York   | https/http | ny.rpc.blockrush.io     |
| 🇩🇪 Frankfurt  | https/http | fra.rpc.blockrush.io    |
| 🇯🇵 Tokyo      | https/http | jp.rpc.blockrush.io     |


<Note>
  While our endpoints support both `HTTPS` and `HTTP` protocols, `HTTP` typically delivers better performance due to lower overhead. Consider using `HTTP` for scenarios where maximum throughput is critical.
</Note>
