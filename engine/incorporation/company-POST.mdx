---
title: "Create a company"
openapi: "POST /public/company"
---

Creates a new company for partner and legacy affiliate incorporation workflows.

<Info>
For the Partner Incorporation API, this creates the Commenda company container that owns incorporations and Commenda OS resources. Save the returned `companyId`; you will use it in `POST /partner/incorporation/companies/{companyId}/incorporations` and in company-scoped Commenda OS endpoints such as `POST /partner/commenda-os/companies/{companyId}/persons`.
</Info>

After creating the company, call `GET /partner/incorporation/jurisdiction-catalog` and `GET /partner/incorporation/requirements` to choose the incorporation jurisdiction and understand the required data.
